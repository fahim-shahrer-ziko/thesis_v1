Task title: Instruction 2

<task definition>
    Estimate the time of the next taxi ride a user is likely to take based on their historical rides, current trip details, and personal mobility habits.
    ________________________________________
    <persona>
    <persona> outlines the user's mobility pattern as a 7-element tuple:
    (Total_Taxi_Rides, Active_Days, Unique_Dropoff_Locations, Top_Destination, Avg_Ride_Distance,
    Peak_Ride_Hours, Weekend_Rides)
    - Total_Taxi_Rides: total number of taxi rides over the observed period.
    - Active_Days: number of distinct days with at least one taxi ride.
    - Unique_Dropoff_Locations: number of distinct drop-off locations.
    - Top_Destination: the most common destination.
    - Avg_Ride_Distance: average distance traveled per ride.
    - Peak_Ride_Hours: hours with the highest taxi ride frequency.
    - Weekend_Rides: number of rides taken on weekends.
    ________________________________________
    <data description>
    <history> lists past taxi rides; <context> shows recent rides leading up to the prediction target. Both are in chronological order. Each ride is a 7-element tuple:
    (Gender, Age_On_June_2024, Entry_DT, Exit_DT, DayName, Pick_Up_Location, Drop_Off_Location)
    The prediction target <target_ride> has the same format but with the drop-off location hidden:
    (Gender, Age_On_June_2024, <entry_time>, None, DayName, Pick_Up_Location, None)
    Only <entry_time> must be predicted.
    ________________________________________
    <thinking guidance>
    Use the following method to predict the most likely entry time:
    1. Examine <history> for repeated pick-up and drop-off locations matching the <target_ride>.
    2. Review <context> for the most recent ride trends.
    3. Compare the user's demographics and current ride context with past patterns.
    4. Apply <persona> to understand the user's usual ride times and destinations.
    5. Combine all insights to generate the best prediction.
    Provide your answer in the required JSON format.
