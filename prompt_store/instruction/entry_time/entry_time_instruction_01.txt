Task title: Instruction 1

<task definition>
    Your goal is to forecast the exact day and time of a person's next bus trip. Utilize the individual's historical bus usage patterns, immediate prior trips, and daily routines to deduce the precise time they are likely to board the bus.
    ________________________________________
    <persona>
    <persona> represents the individual's habitual bus usage as a 7-element tuple:
    (Total_Bus_Trips, Active_Days, Unique_Routes, Favorite_Routes, Avg_Ride_Time,
    Peak_Ride_Days, Weekday_Rides)
    - Total_Bus_Trips: total number of bus rides recorded.
    - Active_Days: number of distinct days with at least one bus ride.
    - Unique_Routes: number of distinct bus routes taken.
    - Favorite_Routes: top 3 most frequently taken bus routes.
    - Avg_Ride_Time: average duration of each bus ride.
    - Peak_Ride_Days: days with the highest frequency of bus rides.
    - Weekday_Rides: number of rides taken on weekdays.
    ________________________________________
    <data description>
    <history> includes past bus trip records; <context> includes recent trips just before the prediction target. Both are in chronological order. Each trip is a 7-element tuple:
    (Gender, Age_On_June_2024, Entry_DT, Exit_DT, DayName, Boarding_Stop_Name, Alighting_Stop_Name)
    The prediction target <target_trip> has the same format but with the exit stop hidden:
    (Gender, Age_On_June_2024, <entry_time>, None, DayName, Boarding_Stop_Name, None)
    Only <entry_time> needs to be predicted.
    ________________________________________
    <thinking guidance>
    Follow these steps to determine the most likely entry time:
    1. Review <history> for patterns in bus route usage, especially those matching the <target_trip>'s Boarding_Stop_Name.
    2. Analyze <context> for any trends in recent bus usage.
    3. Consider demographic factors such as age and gender.
    4. Factor in the average ride time and typical ride duration.
    5. Use <persona> to weigh the importance of frequent vs. less frequent routes.
    6. Synthesize all information to form a reasoned prediction.
    Provide the result in the required JSON format.
